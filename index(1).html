Codex V

Field Operations

Operation · Deep Learning · 2022–2023

DeepWeight — Food Weight Billing System

A semi-autonomous billing system for grocery store food sections using a CNN model (ResNet-152) trained with fast.ai. The system detects food type via camera, collects weight from a load cell, and prints a billing label — achieving 99.22% accuracy after 5 training epochs. Uses weight-cell readings to trigger the system instead of conventional image sensing, reducing false triggers and improving efficiency.

Codex VI

Research & Publication

IEEE Conference Publication · 2023
DeepWeight: A Semi-Autonomous Food Weight Billing System using Deep Learning
2023 International Conference on Control, Communication and Computing (ICCC) · Pages 1–6
Ashley Titus · Mahesh U S · Nandu Krishnan G · Dr. Mercelin Francis

DeepWeight addresses the inefficiency of manual billing in grocery food sections by deploying a CNN model built on ResNet-152 architecture. The system uses Python3, fast.ai, a USB camera, a load cell module, a printer, and a Raspberry Pi 3. It achieves 99.22% accuracy after 5 training epochs and employs weight-cell triggered activation — a novel approach that reduces billing time, avoids false triggers, and improves store efficiency.
